Simplicity and Work

The challenges that come along with our jobs is often one of the leading causes of stress in our lives. Frustration, ruining of relationship with others, and burnout are some of issues stress leads to. Studies have also shown that our personal surroundings indirectly determines or leads to whether or not we live a healthy life. It's something people are either oblivious to, don't decorate, or over do it when they do, but applying simplicity to something as mundane as decorating our personal surroundings can go a very long way in our quest to live a healthy and happy life.
