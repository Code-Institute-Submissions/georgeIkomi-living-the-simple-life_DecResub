About Me

My name is Andrew and living the simple life saved my life

Unconsciously, we engage with a lot of things in life. In so doing, we often fail to reap the true benefits of keeping things as simple as possible in our daily lives.

Heartbreak, with a combination of other factors meant that I was at a very low point in my life.

How I transformed things to my advantage

It was almost lights out for me, had to move into a smaller apartment after selling my old place. Most of my remaining belongings were disposed off all, on top of quitting my job.

It was a really challenging time for me as everything was chaotic. Fortunately for me it all panned out well in the end.

I am now living a simple and stress free life

Adopting some of the ideas shared on the platform really uplifted my outlook towards life, and I no longer feel choked up with stress.

All the improved benefits I feel in my life now is why the idea of floating this site came to mind. I really want to tell the world how I did it and how it can benefit our lives!
